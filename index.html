Built so you can own your vocabulary

The story behind My Dictionary - and why understanding words, not just collecting them, leads to real confidence when you speak.

My name is Alexander, and I'm the creator of My Dictionary. Four years ago, I was learning English and often felt like an outsider - I couldn't find the right word when it mattered. Generic dictionaries didn't help me really understand or remember words.

I wanted to speak without hesitation. So I started building understanding through meanings and context - writing definitions that explained words, not just translated them. That created a deeper connection to the language and made me feel like I belonged.

But doing this manually was exhausting. So I built My Dictionary to make that journey accessible to everyone: capture words from life, understand them, practice until they stick, and see your progress. So you can walk into any room and choose the right word.

Today, My Dictionary helps thousands of people move from hesitation to confidence. The goal is simple: don't just learn words. Own them.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other dictionary apps?
Most apps just translate. We help you own words - so you can walk into any room and choose the right one. You capture words from life, understand them in context, and the app makes sure the ones that trip you up become the ones you reach for. It's a personal vocabulary that grows with you.
What languages are supported?
135+ languages. Build your vocabulary in any language you're learning - English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Hindi, Russian, and many more.
Can I use it offline?
Yes. Your vocabulary is stored locally and syncs when you're online. You can review and practice offline; AI-powered features need internet for generation.
Is there a web version?
Currently available on iOS and macOS. Web version coming soon! Your vocabulary syncs across all your devices.
What subscription options are available?
We offer Weekly ($3.99), Monthly ($4.99), and Yearly ($39.99) subscriptions. There are no free trials or introductory discounts. All plans include all features. Prices may differ depending on your region; prices shown are base U.S. prices.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your device settings. No questions asked, no hidden fees. Your subscription continues until the end of the current billing period.
