Built for Vocabulary Organization

The story behind My Dictionary and why organizing vocabulary matters more than just collecting words.

My name is Alexander, and I'm the creator of My Dictionary. Four years ago, I was learning English and struggling to organize all the new words I encountered. Generic dictionaries didn't help me remember words, and word lists were too disorganized.

I needed a better way to collect, organize, and practice vocabulary. Instead of just translating words, I started building understanding through meanings and context. I would write definitions to explain words, not just translate them. This created a deeper, more natural understanding of vocabulary.

But doing this manually was exhausting. So I built My Dictionary to automate this process and make it accessible to everyone. A powerful tool that lets you collect, organize, sort, and categorize vocabulary exactly how you need it.

Today, My Dictionary helps thousands of people organize their vocabulary journey. The goal is simple: stop collecting words you forget, and start organizing the ones that matter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?
Yes! My Dictionary is free to download with core features including word lookup, vocabulary saving, basic quizzes, and iCloud sync. Upgrade to Pro for advanced features like AI-powered learning and music lessons.
How is this different from other dictionary apps?
Most dictionary apps just translate words. My Dictionary helps you organize and master vocabulary. Add words with multiple meanings, organize with tags, practice with quizzes, and discover vocabulary through music and stories. It's a complete vocabulary management system, not just a dictionary.
Do I need to know music to use this?
Not at all! You just need to enjoy music. Pick any song you already listen to, and the app turns it into a language lesson. No musical knowledge required.
What languages are supported?
50+ languages! Build your vocabulary in any language - English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Hindi, Russian, and many more. The app supports vocabulary in any language, not just English.
Can I use it offline?
Yes! Core features work offline. Your vocabulary is stored locally and syncs when you're online. Music lessons require internet for initial generation, then work offline.
Is there a web version?
Currently available on iOS and macOS. Web version coming soon! Your vocabulary syncs across all your devices.
How does the music vocabulary discovery work?
Choose a song you love. The app analyzes the lyrics, highlights vocabulary, provides context-rich definitions, explains idioms, and creates interactive quizzes. It's a great way to discover new words in context. (Pro feature)
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Absolutely! Cancel anytime from your device settings. No questions asked, no hidden fees. Your subscription continues until the end of the billing period.
