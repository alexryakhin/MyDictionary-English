Understand, Don't Just Translate

The story behind My Dictionary and why understanding beats translation every time.

My name is Alexander, and I'm the creator of My Dictionary. Four years ago, I was struggling to learn English as a Russian speaker with no English-speaking environment around me. I was tired of traditional translation-based learning methods that never seemed to stick.

Then I discovered a powerful concept: learn every language as if it's your first. Instead of constantly translating between languages, I started building understanding through meanings and context. I would write definitions in Russian to explain English words, not translate them. This created a deeper, more natural understanding.

But doing this manually in textbooks was exhausting. So I built My Dictionary to automate this process and make it accessible to everyone. As my English improved, I naturally transitioned to writing definitions in English itself, building true fluency.

Today, I'm fluent in English and use it as my primary language for daily life. I'm proud of this journey and want every language learner to experience the same transformation.

Why Understanding Beats Translation

Deep Learning: Understanding meanings creates lasting knowledge, not just word-for-word memorization.
Natural Fluency: You learn to think in the target language, not constantly translate in your head.
Context Mastery: You grasp how words work in different situations, not just their dictionary equivalents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?
Yes! My Dictionary is free to download with core features including word lookup, vocabulary saving, basic quizzes, and iCloud sync. Upgrade to Pro for advanced features like AI-powered learning and music lessons.
How is this different from other dictionary apps?
Most dictionary apps just translate words. My Dictionary helps you understand meaning in context, learn through music, master idioms, and get personalized AI-powered lessons. It's a complete learning platform, not just a dictionary.
Do I need to know music to use this?
Not at all! You just need to enjoy music. Pick any song you already listen to, and the app turns it into a language lesson. No musical knowledge required.
What languages are supported?
47 languages! Learn any language pair - English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Hindi, Russian, and 41 more. The app supports learning any language, not just English.
Can I use it offline?
Yes! Core features work offline. Your vocabulary is stored locally and syncs when you're online. Music lessons require internet for initial generation, then work offline.
Is there a web version?
Currently available on iOS, macOS, and Android. Web version coming soon! Your data syncs across all devices.
How does the music learning work?
Choose a song you love. The app analyzes the lyrics, highlights vocabulary, provides context-rich definitions, explains grammar, and creates interactive quizzes. It's a complete 5-stage learning experience.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Absolutely! Cancel anytime from your device settings. No questions asked, no hidden fees. Your subscription continues until the end of the billing period.
