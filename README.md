## üìö Learning & Practice Tools

### **Six Types of Quizzes**

1. **Spelling Quiz**
   - See the definition, type the word
   - Perfect for improving spelling skills

2. **Choose Definition Quiz**
   - Multiple choice definitions
   - Test your understanding of word meanings

3. **Sentence Writing Quiz**
   - Write a sentence using the word
   - AI checks if your sentence is correct and makes sense

4. **Context Multiple Choice Quiz**
   - Fill in the blank with multiple choice options
   - Learn how words are used in context

5. **Fill in the Blank Quiz**
   - Type the correct word to complete the sentence
   - Great for learning word usage

6. **Pronunciation Practice**
   - Record yourself saying words or sentences
   - Get feedback on your pronunciation
   - Improve your speaking skills

### **Spaced Repetition System (SRS)**
- **Anki SM-2 Algorithm**: Scientifically-proven spaced repetition
- **Automatic Scheduling**: Words are automatically scheduled for review based on performance
- **Review Queue**: See all words due for review in one place
- **Adaptive Learning**: Difficult words appear more frequently
- **Progress Tracking**: Track your review intervals and ease factors
- **Daily Goals**: Set daily review goals (5-50 words)
- **Review Reminders**: Get notifications when words are due for review

### **Smart Practice Features**
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: The app learns which words you struggle with

- **Customizable Sessions**: Choose word count, quiz types, and filters
- **Tag-Based Practice**: Practice words from specific tags or languages
- **Streak Tracking**: Maintain learning streaks and track your longest practice runs

### **Story Lab** üìñ

## üìñ The Story Behind My Dictionary

Four years ago, I was learning English and needed a better way to organize all the new words I encountered. Generic dictionaries didn't help me remember words, and word lists were too disorganized. I built My Dictionary to solve this: a powerful tool that lets you collect, organize, sort, and categorize vocabulary exactly how you need it.

Now the app helps thousands of people organize their vocabulary learning journey. The goal is simple: **stop collecting words you forget, and start organizing the ones that matter.**
