Privacy Policy
Last updated: November 2025

Introduction

Welcome to My Dictionary! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our vocabulary learning app.

Key Points: We only collect data necessary for app functionality, we never sell your personal information, and you have full control over your data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Display Name: Optional name for personalization
  • Authentication Data: Secure login credentials via Google/Apple Sign-In

App Usage Data

  • Vocabulary Words: Words you add to your dictionary
  • Learning Progress: Quiz results and study patterns
  • App Preferences: Settings and customization choices
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version

AI Content & Lesson Data

  • Prompts & Inputs: Words, sentences, or topics you submit for AI-powered definitions, quizzes, and stories
  • AI Responses: Structured outputs generated by OpenAI models (definitions, stories, lessons, recommendations)
  • Personalization Context: Optional onboarding profile data (learning goals, interests, preferred study languages) used to tailor AI results

Music & Listening Data

  • Apple Music Authorization: Whether you granted MusicKit permissions
  • Song Metadata: Title, artist, album art, and duration for songs you explore or play
  • Lesson Context: Lyrics excerpts and CEFR levels required to build MusicKit lessons

Diagnostic & Analytics Data

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous identifiers needed to secure your session
  • Crash & Performance Logs: High-level device diagnostics with no personal content
  • Subscription Status: RevenueCat identifiers and purchase receipts needed to validate premium access

Shared Dictionary Data

  • Collaborative Content: Words shared in collaborative dictionaries
  • Collaborator Information: Email addresses of dictionary collaborators
  • Collaboration Activity: Contributions and interactions within shared dictionaries

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Provide vocabulary learning features
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Enable collaborative dictionary features
  • Personalize your learning experience

Improvement & Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new learning tools and features
  • Optimize app performance

Communication

  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Share collaboration invitations

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, remote configuration, and secure hosting
  • RevenueCat: Subscription validation across Apple and Google storefronts
  • OpenAI: Processes AI prompts and generates structured learning content
  • Apple Music / MusicKit: Provides licensed audio playback, catalog metadata, and MusicKit lesson assets
  • Google / Apple Sign-In: Federated authentication options

Collaborative Features

  • Email addresses are shared only with dictionary collaborators
  • Shared dictionary content is visible to all collaborators
  • You control who can access your shared dictionaries

How Third-Party Services Handle Your Data

We rely on trusted vendors to power premium experiences. Each partner only receives the minimum data needed to perform its role.

OpenAI

  • Receives your prompts, selected words, and personalization context required to generate educational responses
  • Does not receive your email address or billing information
  • Responses are stored in your account solely for in-app usage history

Firebase

  • Provides secure authentication, analytics, and remote configuration
  • Stores hashed identifiers, device metadata, and crash diagnostics‚Äînever your saved vocabulary content
  • Helps us detect abuse, maintain uptime, and deliver critical updates

RevenueCat

  • Receives app store receipts and anonymous user IDs to manage subscriptions
  • Does not process your vocabulary data or AI content
  • Keeps entitlements synced across iOS, macOS, and Android

Apple Music / MusicKit

  • Requires an active Apple Music subscription and MusicKit authorization from you
  • Receives catalog identifiers for songs you search, preview, or study
  • Playback occurs through MusicKit; we do not download or store full audio files

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with industry-standard security
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Be careful when sharing dictionary access
  • Log out when using shared devices

Your Rights & Choices

Data Access

  • View and download your data anytime
  • Export your vocabulary and progress
  • Access your account information

Data Control

  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Remove yourself from shared dictionaries
  • Opt out of analytics and non-essential data collection
  • Control notification preferences

Account Management

  • Update your profile information
  • Change privacy settings
  • Manage subscription preferences

Data Retention

Active Accounts

We retain your data as long as your account is active and you continue to use our services.

Account Deletion

  • Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Shared dictionary content may remain if other collaborators are active
  • Backup data is permanently deleted within 90 days

Legal Requirements

We may retain certain information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Children's Privacy

My Dictionary is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Firebase, OpenAI, RevenueCat, and Apple Music may route requests through the United States or other regions. We rely on contractual safeguards, encryption in transit, and industry-standard protections to keep your information secure.

AI Content Controls

You can disable AI personalization at any time from the in-app privacy controls. Disabling personalization prevents onboarding profile data from being sent to OpenAI, though anonymous prompts are still required to deliver AI-powered features.

MusicKit Permissions

If you connect Apple Music, we request MusicKit authorization tokens from Apple to verify your subscription. We never access your personal library‚Äîonly catalog media IDs for the songs you choose to study. You can revoke MusicKit access through your Apple device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying an in-app notification

Your continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
