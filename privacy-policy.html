Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

In short

  • We do not collect your words, account info, or learning progress.
  • We collect only anonymous analytics and purchase history (for subscription validation).
  • We don’t sell your data and we don’t show ads.
  • Your vocabulary and progress stay on your device (or in your own sync, if the app supports it).

What we collect

  • Anonymous analytics: usage patterns, app version, device type, OS version, crash reports - no personal identifiers.
  • Purchase history: subscription status and purchase receipts via the App Store so we can validate your access to paid features.

What we don’t collect

  • Your vocabulary words or dictionary content
  • Account details (email, name, sign-in credentials)
  • Learning progress or study data
  • Any content you create in the app

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell or rent your data.
  • We don’t share your data with third parties except for anonymous analytics and subscription validation (see below).

Third parties we use

  • Analytics: anonymous usage and crash data to improve the app. No personal data is sent.
  • App Store: purchase and subscription validation only. Apple processes receipts to confirm your subscription status. Family Sharing is supported.

Your control

  • You can limit or block analytics via your device or network settings.
  • Purchase data is held by Apple; manage subscriptions and Family Sharing in your Apple ID settings.

Children

We don’t aim the app at under-13s and don’t knowingly collect their personal data. If you think we have, contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post the new version in the app and, for big changes, notify you there or by email. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? hello@mydictionary.org
